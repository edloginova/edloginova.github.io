Teaching

I am passionate about developing new standars for STEM education. Trends like online education are giving access to high quality education content to all people despite their background or financial resources. And I believe that artificial intelligence can aid studying process and become a next step in the evolution of teaching. We can use chatbots to interact with students, remind them about their tasks and answer their questions. Another idea is to track their progress by creating analytical dashboards and help them build an optimal learning track.

Through my work as a teaching assistant and a tutor, I have developed the ability to explain sophisticated concepts and strengthened my communication skills.

One of my courseworks involved creating animated visualizations of interesting examples for calculus theorems. You can check them out here.

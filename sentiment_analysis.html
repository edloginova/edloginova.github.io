Sentiment analysis

Also known as opinion mining, this is an essential element of text analysing systems. The goal is to understand the mood of the text: is it positive or negative? A more fine-grained analysis includes extracting an emotion - anger, suprise, happiness, etc.

I am currently doing a thesis internship in Vrije Universite Brussel, Artifical Intelligence laboratory. The internship is situated within the ODyCCEuS project (Opinion Dynamics and Cultural Conflict in European Spaces). The ODyCCEuS-project is funded by the European Union and will develop opinion mining tools to study how social and cultural conflicts are born and spread through online media. Our team is working on creating a labeled corpus consisting of comments to newspaper articles on climate change. We are going to compare existing sentiment analysis tools, as well as create our own models.

Besides, I am going to join a great team of Belgian startup Musimap for a summer internship. They are developing a scalable, market ready music intelligence platform that will provide clients with personalized content. My work will concern sentiment analysis of lyrics to enhance the granular proprietary database with the information encoded in words of the songs.

1A: Social Applications

Privacy-preserving Neural Representations of Text. Private demographic data. Unlearn something in a model: make it predictive of the main task such as sentiment and not predictive of sensitive information (protected attributes) by the attacker. A solution is to reverse gradient or use multiple adversaries.
DeClarE: Debunking Fake News and False Claims using Evidence-Aware Deep Learning. Kashyap Popat, Subhabrata Mukherjee, Andrew Yates and Gerhard Weikum Fake news is top ten issues by the world economic forum. Article and claim source embeddings. PCA clearly separates fake and authentic sources. It also separates sources into political ideologies. Datasets: Snopes, politifact, NewsTrust. Other approaches: distant sup (manual features and lexicons)
It's going to be okay: Measuring Access to Support in Online Communities. Zijian Wang and David Jurgens
Detecting Gang-Involved Escalation on Social Media Using Context. Serina Chang, Ruiqi Zhong, Ethan Adams, Fei-Tzin Lee, Siddharth Varia, Desmond Patton, William Frey, Chris Kedzie and Kathy McKeow. Half life ratio to model decay in history of tweets. Location and domain specific word embeddings.

9B: Sentiment I

Cross-topic Argument Mining from Heterogeneous Sources. Christian Stab, Tristan Miller, Benjamin Schiller, Pranav Rai and Iryna Gurevych. Topic can be expressed with keywords. Argument is a span of text with the evidence for or against. Labels pro, con, no argument. Incorporate information abort the topic with the contextual LSTM (add it to c and i gates). Multi-task learning with auxiliary data. UKP Sentential Argument Mining Corpus. www.argumentsearch.com<
Summarizing Opinions: Aspect Extraction Meets Sentiment Prediction and They Are Both Weakly Supervised. Stefanos Angelidis and Mirella Lapata. Weak supervision: no supervision for aspects of sentiment and no gold summaries. ABAE to discover topics and link them to aspects - discovery. Inject prior knowledge via seed words, no ability to discover new aspects - extraction MATE. We can add multi task learning to make aspect words domain specific. Opinion salience. Best worst scaling.

9D: Semantics VI

A Word-Complexity Lexicon and A Neural Readability Ranking Model for Lexical Simplification. Mounica Maddela and Wei Xu. Pairwise neural ranking model plus human rankings of word complexity. Before people used word length, frequency and language model probability. But assumption of a shorter word being easier is wrong 21% time. Same with frequency, 14%. 6 point likert scale for annotators worked much better than 5 point.
Hierarchical Quantized Representations for Script Generation. Noah Weber, Leena Shekhar, Niranjan Balasubramanian and Nate Chambers. Script variables. VQVAE.
Semantic Role Labeling for Learner Chinese: the Importance of Syntactic Parsing and L2-L1 Parallel Data. Zi Lin, Yuguang Duan, Yuanyuan Zhao, Weiwei Sun and Xiaojun Wan.Interlanguage: L2 preserving some features of L1. Corpus based on language exchange social networking services. Pay attention to semantics to still understand despite mistakes.

A Skeleton-Based Model for Promoting Coherence Among Sentences in Narrative Story Generation.. Jingjing Xu, Xuancheng Ren, Yi Zhang, Qi Zeng, Xiaoyan Cai and Xu Sun
NEXUS Network: Connecting the Preceding and the Following in Dialogue Generation. .Xiaoyu Shen, Hui Su, Wenjie Li and Dietrich Klakow. Maximize mutual information between the current utterance and the surrounding context to generate a good nexus and not a universal reply.

A Co-Attention Neural Network Model for Emotion Cause Analysis with Emotional Context Awareness. Xiangju Li, Kaisong Song, Shi Feng, Daling Wang and Yifei Zhang.
